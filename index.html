An Adverse Outcome Pathway, informally applied

The role calls for AOP-informed mechanistic assessment. Here is one — built the same way I'd build one for a crop protection compound, just with a more familiar test species (myself). Two stressors converge on a shared key event, a modulating factor buffers the response, and the sliders below let you explore the exposure space.

Running load follows a hormetic (non-monotonic) dose-response — moderate load builds resilience, too little or too much both reduce capacity. Heat is a straightforward monotonic stressor above a threshold. Toggle the modulating factor to see how it shifts the curve.
